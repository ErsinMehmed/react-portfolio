# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and technical peers evaluating Ersin Hyusein as a senior full-stack web developer. They land here from a CV, LinkedIn, or a job application link, scan for 30-60 seconds, and decide whether to read further or move on. Bilingual audience (Bulgarian, English).

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that proves technical seniority: real production projects (myPOS, freelance e-commerce), a deep resume timeline, and a certifications trail. Success = a visitor walks away convinced this person ships production-grade work and gets in touch or downloads the CV.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, technical. The interface should read like the work it describes: clean, deliberate, no filler. Confidence through restraint and craft, not through hype copy or decoration.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS/AI-template look: hero-metric blocks, gradient text, uniform icon+heading+text card grids, stock "modern startup" blue-and-white, prayer-hands illustrations. Nothing that reads as a template with the name swapped in.

## Design Principles

- Show, don't tell: production project details and real metrics carry more weight than adjectives.
- Craft as the pitch: the site's own polish is evidence of the skill it's advertising.
- Bilingual parity: every surface must read as intentional in both EN and BG, not like a translated afterthought.
- Restraint over decoration: confidence expressed through precision and whitespace, not gradients or ornament.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA: color contrast, keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic markup.
